American Lung Association
The American Lung Association strongly supports help ensure all patients facing lung cancer get the highest standard of care and treatment. Helping patients understand and navigate treatment guidelines is one important way to empower them to get the care they want and need. We are proud to endorse the National Comprehensive Cancer Network (NCCN) Guidelines for Patients with Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer.
