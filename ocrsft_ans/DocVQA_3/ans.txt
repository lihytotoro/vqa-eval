Title: 5 January 1961

Subtitle: Dear Doctor Lorans

Body Text:
Thank you indeed for your letter of 13 December and the accompanying history of the Southern Medical Association. I am pleased indeed to have a copy of this. May I you on the vast amount of interesting information which you have detailed in this history.

With my very best thanks and all good wishes for 1961.

Sincerely,
William J. Darby
