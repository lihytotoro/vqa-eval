Title: The Sugar Association, Inc.

Address: 1511 K Street, N.W. Washington, D.C. 20005

Date: August 15, 1978

Body Text:
TO:
PUBLICATIONS COMMUNICATIONS COMMITTEE

Gentlemen:

With organization of the Nutritive Sweetener Council, we have made some personnel changes to provide additional help here, we believe, improve our efficiency.

Marian Rahal, a Byou employee who worked for the Association out of Byou’s New York office, has moved to another account.

Nancy Gravett, who is situated in this office, has taken Marian Rahal’s place in Byou and the Associated Insurance Agents (Washington, D.C.), a trade association, where she was Manager of Media Relations. Prior to that, she was Director of Media Relations for Eureka College in Illinois. She is a graduate of the University of Illinois, with a degree in journalism.

Kristine Meyer has joined the Council as Secretary/Assistant. She is a newcomer to Washington, having recently received her degree in journalism from West Virginia University.

Cordially,
J.R. O’Connell
Director of Public Relations
