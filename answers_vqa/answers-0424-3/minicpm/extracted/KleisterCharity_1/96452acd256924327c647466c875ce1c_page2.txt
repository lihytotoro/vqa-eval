Title: ROBERT MONK'S FONTAINT CHARITY
Subtitle: CHARTER REGISTER NUMBER: 234281

Header: ANNUAL REPORT

Section Title: ACCOUNTS YEAR ENDED 31 DECEMBER 2014

Body Text:
- Robert Monk's Foxton Charity is registered with the Charity Commission for England and Wales as an Unincorporated Charity with the governing document of a scheme dated 16 December 2017 plus subsequent variations.
- The Charity owns the land and buildings known as the Robert Monk Hall and Recreation Ground in Foxton, Leicestershire.
- The Charity is administered by the Trustees listed on page 1 of all whom served throughout the year 2014 are appointed by each appointed Foxton Parish Council.
- Ex-officio trustees, namely the local District Council, Vicar of Foxton and Churchwardens. At present there is one trustee vacancy which is a churchwarden.
- The Charity pays the remuneration of the trustee.
- The Trustees held seven meetings during 2014.

Section Title: OBJECTS OF THE CHARITY

Body Text:
- The objects of the Charity are to maintain the village hall and adjacent recreation ground in the parish of Foxton for operational and to provide loans and grants as permitted.

Section Title: ACHIEVEMENTS AND PERFORMANCE

Body Text:
- The hall is available for hire by local charities and individual groups. As a result of the online booking facility, the number of bookings and value of packages increased during 2014. The addition of the weekend party package on the website has also helped to attract more wedding bookings. There has also been an increase in the number of regular account holders.

Section Title: NO REQUESTS FOR LOAN

Body Text:
- No requests for loan were received by the charity during 2014. Grants of £250 were awarded.
- £480 was paid from the Coal Fund as winter fuel allowance for Foxton residents.

Section Title: RESERVES

Body Text:
- The reserves are kept at £102,80.

Section Title: GRANT-MAKING AND LOANING

Body Text:
- Applications for grants and loans are considered by the trustees. They are only available to Foxton residents.
- 99% was invested in COIF Charities Fund and the balance was held in COIF Charities Deposit Fund.
