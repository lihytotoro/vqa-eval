Title: PEACEKEEPING in Mali

Subtitle: The United Nations Multidimensional Integrated Stabilization Mission in Mali (MINUSMA) was established on 25 April 2013 to support political processes and carry out a number of security-related tasks. However, since its launch on 1 July 2013, peacekeepers have been victims of various attacks, including ambush, suicide bombing, shelling, indirect fire and IEDs.

Table:

- 1,371 Explosive Remnants of War DESTROYED
- 3 MILLION sq meters of land CLEARED

Statistics:
