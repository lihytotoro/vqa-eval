Section Title: CONSTITUTION OF THE CHARITY
Body Text: Robert Monk’s Foxton Charity is registered with the Charity Commission for England and Wales as an Unincorporated Charity with the governing document of a scheme dated 16 December 2017 plus subsequent variations.

Section Title: The Charity owns the land and buildings known as the Robert Monk Hall Village and Recreation Ground in Foxton, Leicestershire.

Section Title: The Charity is administered by the Trustee List on page 1 of all whom served throughout the year 2 trustees are appointed by each appointed Foxton Parish Council ex-officio, namely the local District Council, Vicar of Foxton and Churchwardens. At present there is one trustee vacancy.

Body Text: The Charity pays the remuneration of the trustees.

Section Title: OBJECTIVES OF THE CHARITY
Body Text: The objects of the Charity are to maintain the hall and adjacent recreation ground in the parish of Foxton for operational use and to provide loans and grants as permitted.

Section Title: ACHIEVEMENTS AND PERFORMANCE
Body Text: The hall is available for hire by the charity and individual groups. As a result of the online booking facility, the number and value of bookings increased by 4. During the addition of the weekend package on the website has also helped to attract more wedding bookings. There has also been an increase in the number of regular account hirers.

Section Title: NO REQUESTS FOR LOANS
Body Text: No requests for loans were received by the charity during 2014. Grants of £250 were awarded to various residents for winter fuel.

Section Title: VARIOUS POLICIES
Body Text: The unrestricted funds are kept at a £108,250. These are kept at a relatively high level due to the age of the village hall.

Section Title: Grant-making and loans
Body Text: Applications for grants and loans are considered by the trustees. They are only available to Foxton residents.

Section Title: Investment selection
Body Text: 99.9% was invested in COIF Charity Fund and the balance was held in COIF Charities Deposit Fund.

Section Title: The Trustees regularly review the major risks to which the charity is exposed and are satisfied that systems are in place to mitigate exposure.
