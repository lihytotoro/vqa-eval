Title: PEACEKEEPING in Mali

Subtitle: The United Nations Multidimensional Integrated Stabilization Mission in Mali (MINUSMA) was established on 25 April 2013 to support political processes and carry out a number of security-related tasks, since its launch on 1 July 2013, MINUSMA has been victims of various types of attacks, including ambush, suicide bombing, shelling, indirect fire and IEDs.

Body Text:
- 14 out of 24 COURTS & PRISONS with MINUSMA's support
- 7,389 Malian Security Forces trained by MINUSMA
- TRAINED by MINUSMA
- 1,371 EXPLOSIVE REMOVALS of WAR WEAPONS
- 35 KILLERS in attacks
- 138 WOUNDED due to hostile acts
- 80% of MINUSMA troops deployed in the NORTH
- MILITARY 9,142
- POLICE 1,180
